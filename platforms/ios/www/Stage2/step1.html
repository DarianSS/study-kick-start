Managing Head Stuff

So you found out that thoughts such as (students choice of thoughts from stage 1, step 1, page 8 page ) are a big component to why you struggle. But what can you do about them?

Try this exercise: Imagine a cartoon elephant. It’s a pink elephant and has red painted toenails and red polka dot bow on it's head. It has a big smile hidden under it's trunk and big blue eyes. Can you see it..? Take your time, don't rush. You will have 30 seconds to build a really clear picture of the elephant.

Did it look anything like this? OK, now spend another 30 seconds merging this image with the one you had in your mind.

Now you'll have another 30 seconds to complete the task. Time will start as soon as you move onto the next screen where you will also find the instructions of what you need to do. Keeping the elephant in mind, press next when you are ready to move to the final task.

DO NOT THINK ABOUT THE PINK ELEPHANT!

Chances are that as much as you tried not to let it happen, the pink elephant kept popping back into your mind. Did you manage to get through the whole 30 seconds without thinking about the pink elephant?

  • Yes
  • No

When you try to rid yourself of your thoughts about how much work you have to do or your ability to do it by ignoring those thoughts and pushing then away you are actually programming your mind to think about it more.
