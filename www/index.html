Study Kick Start

Whether you find yourself lacking in motivation or engaging in full on procrastination, Study Kick Start will help you to understand its causes, what keeps you locked in and, most importantly, how to combat it.

You’ll be moving step-by-step, through proven methods for getting your work done with the minimum of stress. And, if the stress still feels too much to handle, don’t panic! You’ll be able to unlock some materials to put in your bonus bag by answering questions throughout your journey.

For each correct answer you’ll get a goodie which will help you to learn ways to manage stress so that you can still get your work done. A winner either way! Stage one captures your personal study struggles and stage two is to help you work through them. Let’s set you up.
