Doing Stuff

The final step in Stage 1 is about what you do in response to your thoughts, feelings and sensations. This matters because what you do will either assist you or hinder you - it is either helpful or unhelpful. You can spot your helpful behaviours as they lead to success in achieving your goals. Your unhelpful behaviours can be spotted as they tend to be repeated without success or accompanied by unwanted consequences/ fallout/ effects/ follow-up.

Tunde went out to parties and ate chocolate; Yolanda avoided looking at her schedule whilst Daniel updated his Facebook profile picture, read a book, played Candy Crush and watched TV.

Some of your unhelpful behaviours are really obvious such as Daniel playing Candy Crush, whilst others can be more difficult to spot.

Chris believes that by lying around and thinking about the assignment some studying has taken place but it doesn't mean that the paper is any closer to being finished. Chris' behaviour is unhelpful as it doesn't actually progress to beginning the creative process of capturing any thoughts on paper.
